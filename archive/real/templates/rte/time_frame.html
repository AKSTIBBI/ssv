RTE Admission Time Frame

The admission process under the Right to Education (RTE) Act follows a specific schedule as prescribed by the Education Department of Rajasthan. The timeline is designed to ensure a transparent and organized admission process for children from disadvantaged groups and economically weaker sections.

Please note that the exact dates may vary slightly each year based on government notifications. The general timeline for the 2023-24 academic session is provided below:

January

Notification and Advertisement

  • Official notification for RTE admissions by the Education Department
  • Advertisement in local newspapers and school notice board
  • Announcement on school website about the commencement of RTE admissions
January - February

Application Process

  • Distribution of application forms at school reception
  • Online application form available on school website
  • Assistance provided to parents in filling application forms
  • Awareness sessions for parents about the RTE admission process
March

Submission and Verification

  • Last date for submission of completed application forms
  • Verification of documents submitted by applicants
  • Preparation of list of eligible candidates
  • Display of eligible candidates list on school notice board and website
March - April

Selection Process

  • Lottery draw (if number of applications exceeds available seats)
  • Announcement of selected candidates
  • Display of selected candidates list on school notice board and website
  • Communication to selected candidates via SMS/email/phone
April

Admission Formalities

  • Completion of admission formalities by selected candidates
  • Submission of original documents for verification
  • Issuance of admission confirmation letter
  • Parent orientation program for newly admitted students
May - June

Second Round (If Required)

  • Second round of admissions for vacant seats (if any)
  • Notification for second round on school notice board and website
  • Selection process for second round candidates
  • Completion of admission formalities for second round candidates
June - July

Commencement of Academic Session

  • Distribution of textbooks, uniforms, and study materials to RTE students
  • Special orientation program for RTE students and their parents
  • Beginning of regular classes for all students
  • Submission of final admission report to the Education Department

Important Notes:

  • All dates are tentative and subject to change as per the directives of the Education Department of Rajasthan.
  • Parents/guardians are advised to regularly check the school notice board, website, and local newspapers for any updates regarding RTE admissions.
  • No application fee is charged for RTE admissions.
  • Applications received after the last date will not be considered under any circumstances.
  • The school reserves the right to verify the authenticity of all documents submitted. In case of any fraudulent information or documents, the admission will be cancelled immediately.
  • For any queries related to RTE admissions, please contact our RTE Coordinator at rte@ssvtrust.com or visit the school office during working hours.
