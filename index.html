Welcome to Shri Siddhi Vinayak Shikshan Sansthan

Best Education is Our Tradition

Shri Siddhi Vinayak Educational Trust, is a strong 7 years old institution and we have understood that education over the years has undergone a metamorphosis. Children today have lot more opportunities and trust have realised that education has to become all important and focused today. Shri Siddhivinayak educational trust is making continuous efforts to connect the poor and unemployed of this area with education since a long time. To educate the economically backward people, it is distributing free books and working to provide them with study material. The main objective of Shri Siddhivinayak education trust is to provide financial help to any poor unemployed person who is not capable of helping his children in the situation of low income so that the children can be connected with education.
